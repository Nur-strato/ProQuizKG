Our Mission

We aim to develop the culture of intellectual competitions among the student community of Kyrgyzstan. Our goal is to create a powerful intercollegiate ecosystem where erudition, logic, and team spirit become the main trends among today's progressive youth.

💡

Intellectual Growth

Popularizing knowledge, sharp logic, and fast thinking beyond the standard university curriculum.

🤝

Unity & Collaboration

Building tight bridges between different universities in Bishkek, creating a strong youth community.

🏆

Fair Play

Ensuring a transparent and honest championship system where every single point is earned through pure intellect.

Our Strategic Goals

  • ✔ To unite students from at least 8 leading universities of the Kyrgyz Republic within a single multi-league tournament system.
  • ✔ To digitize the intellectual sports sector by launching user profiles, live stats, and dynamic leaderboards.
  • ✔ To organize a grand intercollegiate final with high-profile guest support, highlighting the prestige of smart youth.
